Mangrove Beach

The Mangrove Beach provides a spectacular view of the beach and serves as a purpose to conserve the mangroves as best as they can. Not only do they have an amazing landscape of the beach, but plenty of fun activities that can be enjoyed by many; such as the ATV ride.
Mangrove Conservation Site also allows their visitors a chance at making their very own chips. Products which are sold there are: soap, chips or cookies, syrup, dawet or cendo, coffee, batik dye, and much more.
